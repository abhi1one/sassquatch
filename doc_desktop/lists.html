Divided List

List items in dividedList expect block-level elements, like p or img. dividedList and paddedList usually live inside a docSection wrapper.

  • Eggs

  • Milk

  • Beer

    <div class="docBox">
        <div class="docSection">
            <ul class="dividedList">
                <li><p>Eggs</p></li>
                <li><p>Milk</p></li>
                <li><p>Beer</p></li>
            </ul>
        </div>
    </div>
