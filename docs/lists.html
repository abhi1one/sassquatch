Divided List

List items in dividedList expect block-level elements, like p or img. dividedList and paddedList usually live inside a doc-content wrapper.

  • Eggs

  • Milk

  • Beer

    <ul class="dividedList">
        <li><p>Eggs</p></li>
        <li><p>Milk</p></li>
        <li><p>Beer</p></li>
    </ul>

Padded List

The paddedList pattern is the same as dividedList, but without borders.

  • Eggs

  • Milk

  • Beer

    <ul class="dividedList">
        <li><p>Eggs</p></li>
        <li><p>Milk</p></li>
        <li><p>Beer</p></li>
    </ul>
